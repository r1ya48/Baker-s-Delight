ABOUT BAKERS DELIGHT

What we do is nice and simple, it's freshly baked bread! Since 1980, we’ve been committed to delighting our customers and being pillars of our communities. We’ve been baking the same way for 40 years to give you our signature delightful experience. Because you don’t just deserve butter, you deserve the best.

  • GRAINS TO YOUR BREAD

  • You may not realise it, but your bread has undergone an amazing journey well before you take it home and tuck in.
  • Our flour is sourced from Aussie farms, processed by family-owned millers and lovingly crafted into bread by our bakers
  • Take the journey with us.

The rise and rise of Bakers Delight

In 1980, Roger and Lesley Gillespie opened the first Bakers Delight in Hawthorn, Victoria. Since then, we’ve been delighting our customers every day with delicious, freshly baked bread. From our classic loaves to our artisanal sourdoughs, our bread is baked fresh each day using traditional recipes and real ingredients.

Still proudly family-owned and operated by joint CEOs Elise Gillespie & David Christie, Bakers Delight is now Australia’s most successful bakery franchise and can be found in more than 700 locations across Australia, Canada, USA and New Zealand.

  • OUR HISTORY

  • Here’s a timeline of our history in the baking, ever since we first opened our doors in 1980.

1980- Bakers Delight is founded on May 1st

Roger and Lesley Gillespie opened the first Bakers Delight in Hawthorn, Melborne along withe their mate Gary Stephenson. The bakery is still going strong today and the wider Bakers Delight buisness remains in family hands. In 2017, Roger and Lesely appointed their daughter Elise and her husband, David Christie as joint CEOs before taking up positions on the Bakers Delight.

1990- 20 Bkeries operating in Australia

With our Franchising concept firmly in place we had opened 20 bakeries in our thenth year. This number was set to grow significantly in the following years.

1992- The first Bakers Delight TV commercial airs

We release our first ever TV commercial to the public, which showcased our delicious products and wonderful staff. This also marks the first use of our iconic bakers Delight jingle!

1996- We have 225 bakeries open across AUS & NZ

Following a breakthrough growth period across Australia and new Zealand, we reached a total 225 bakeries in operation.

1999- Our partnership with Breast Cancer Network Australia begins

Since 1999, we've raised more than $18 million for the BCNA. A landmark goes directly to the support of thousands od Australians and their families who are affected by breast cancer.

2000- We have 400 bakeries open across AUS & NZ

A continued period of expansion resulted in our number of operating bakeries reaching impressive new heights of over 400.

2004 We win a Prime Minister's Award

We're so proud of our continued support of BCNA and were honoured to recieve the Prime Minister's Award for Excellence in Community Buisness Partnership (multi-site large buisness) from John Howard.

2005- Celebrating our 25th birthday

A quater of a century! it may have felt just like yeaterday that we opened our first bakery in Melborne, but Bakers Delight celebrated turning 25 in style.

2009- Buisness Review weekly recognise our community practise

We were proud finalists for Excellence in Community Practise in the BRW Awards, held every year by the prestigious magazine, Buisness Review Weekly.

2013- Winner of Reader's Digest Most Trusted Brands Award

We put a lot of time and effort into making sure every single slice of bread we produce is up to the same high standard. So we were thrilled you noticed and voted us the winner of the annual Most trusted Brands Award in Reader's Digest.

2016- over 700 Bakeries operating across 4 countries

Baker Delight and COBS bread can now be found in more than 700 locations across Australia, Canada, USA and New Zealand. That;s a lot of bread being baked fresh and a lot of happy customers.

2019- Franchise group of the Year

The Australian Retailers Association names Bakers Delight as the Nation's Best Franchise Buisness for 2019. The prestigious Buisness for 2019. The prestigious awards are an annual celebration of the very best in retailing across Australia and an oppurtunity to shine the spotlight on those buisnesses that excelled throughtout the year.

2020- Celebrating our 40th birthday

How time flies! This year we celebrated the last 40 years and set our sights on the next 40. As Australia's largest bakery franchise, we can't wait to see what the future has in store.
